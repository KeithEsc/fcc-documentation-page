Introduction

Boyle Transportation is a specialized transportation logistics provider delivering exceptional quality, safety and security to select clients in the life sciences and government/defense sectors. Our long-term, exclusive focus on these technical and quality-focused markets has led us to mirror our clients with stringent quality standards, highly trained and dedicated professionals, and continual investment in technology and equipment. Hazardous Materials Expertise

Boyle has a long and proven track record of handling hazardous material transportation in government and defense, including radioactive materials and explosives.

  • Compliance

    • We operate in full compliance with US Department of Transportation (DOT) regulations, including those concerning the movement of hazardous materials. We also follow the recommended Security Action Items from the Transportation Security Administration (TSA). Boyle is approved by the US Department of Defense (DOD) and the Department of Energy (DOE).

  • HAZMAT Transport

    • We act as a partner in risk management and HAZMAT transport protocols. Our safety record is unparalleled in the market, and we continually invest significant resources in training, equipment and safety technology to ensure that your shipment is delivered safely and securely. All of our transportation professionals hold hazardous material endorsements and are trained / certified for HAZMAT transportation.
    • Whether you need to transport nuclear fuel cycle, low-level or mixed waste, or radioisotopes, you can trust our knowledge and experience to handle your shipment in line with applicable laws and regulations. Our Operations Center closely monitors the route of travel for every hazardous waste shipment and provides continuous visibility to you through our IT systems. We offer a range of trailer configurations to suit your hazardous material shipment.
    • The US DOT provides publicly accessible data on carrier safety performance – collected during on-road safety inspections, measuring seven critical areas relating to crashes. You can check out Boyle Transportation and other carriers at https://ai.fmcsa.dot.gov/SMS/Carrier/111440/CompleteProfile.aspx.

  • HAZMAT Shipment Security

    • Safety and security protocols are more vital than ever when it comes to transportation of hazardous materials. Boyle Transportation has long been a leading choice in both government / defense and life science industries. With our continuous internal implementation of extensive hazardous materials training, experienced transportation professionals, state-of-the-art equipment and proven quality systems, we ensure the secure delivery of hazardous materials shipments to wherever they need to go.

  • Hazardous Materials Safety Permit

    • The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requires carriers that transport certain hazardous materials – radioactive materials, explosives and toxic by inhalation materials – in certain volumes to carry a Hazardous Materials Safety Permit (HMSP). Boyle Transportation holds an HMSP permit and complies with all applicable regulations in 49CFR, including training, routing, parking, insuring and placarding. The safety of our transportation professionals, customers and all stakeholders involved in the transport of hazardous materials is our highest priority. To verify our HMSP with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA), see https://safer.fmcsa.dot.gov/mcs150t/pkg_shipper.prc_shipper_request; US DOT Number 111440.
Classified Shipments and Protective Services

Classified shipments require strict compliance measures and constant surveillance by vetted and highly trained staff. Boyle’s extensive track record of transporting classified materials for the government and defense sector makes us exceptionally well equipped to securely handle your highly sensitive shipments.

Our specific capabilities include:

  • Defense Transportation Tracking System (DTTS)
  • Secure handling of sensitive or classified materials to National Industrial Security Program standards
  • Constant Surveillance Service (CIS)
  • Dual Driver Protective Service (DDP)
  • Protective Security Service (PSS)
  • Satellite Motor Surveillance Service (SNS)
  • DTTS Trailer Tracking Service (DCS)
  • Security Escort Vehicle (SEV)
Expedited Service

Expedited service is used when people need to know that their shipment will arrive at its destination within a defined, usually short timeframe – but also safely and securely. There is no better way to achieve this than with Boyle Transportation, where our numerous differentiating characteristics of the business are fundamental to achieving the end goal.

Our overall methodology has resulted in 100% on-time performance for expedited shipments. Since our vehicles are staffed by teams of two transportation professionals, we provide next-day delivery up to 1,000 miles.

We provide door-to-door expedited service throughout the continental USA and Eastern Canada, with assurances that your shipments will arrive safely and on time. Our professional team provides coverage 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  • Shipment Tracking

    • From the moment your shipment is initiated, our Operations Center monitors every detail and provides you with web-based, real-time tracking updates throughout its entire journey, so you know exactly what’s going on and that the shipment is on schedule. Our proprietary route monitoring system provides you with full visibility of your shipment at all times via web-based or mobile app. If you would prefer to speak with someone directly, our Operations Center personnel are also accessible around the clock.

  • Known Entity

    • Unlike most carriers, we do not subcontract shipments to independent owner-operators. All of our transportation professionals are Boyle Transportation employees operating vehicles from our state-of-the-art fleet, controlled by our Operations Center, and with full redundancy back-up. We never send expedited or sensitive shipments any other way or with anyone not trained to our professional standards and credentials.
Cross-Border Shipments (US-Canada)

Boyle manages the various logistics involved in cross-border shipments between the U.S. and Canada, keeping your items moving efficiently and securely across borders. Our transportation professionals maintain close contact with our Operations Center personnel to keep you informed of your shipment status, with full visibility from point to point.

We have been navigating the complexities of cross-border logistics for decades, providing us with a deep understanding of the documentation required to clear customs and leveraging our vast experience to mitigate potential disruptions.

  • Our cross-border solutions include:

    • Process conveyance, equipment and personnel information through ACE/DHS for U.S. freight and through CBSA for Canadian freight
    • Expeditious processing and thorough documentation review to mitigate customs and cross-border issues, helping to reduce costs due to delays and refusals
